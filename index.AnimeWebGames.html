There are numerous anime-inspired games across various platforms, including consoles, PC, and mobile devices. These games cover a wide range of genres, from action and adventure to role-playing and visual novels. Here are some popular anime games that have gained recognition:

  1. Dragon Ball Z: Kakarot (2020):

    2. An action RPG that retells the story of Dragon Ball Z, allowing players to experience iconic moments from the anime.

  2. One Piece: Pirate Warriors series:

    3. These are action-adventure games based on the popular One Piece anime and manga series. Players engage in large-scale battles against hordes of enemies.

  3. Naruto Shippuden: Ultimate Ninja Storm series:

    4. A series of fighting games based on the Naruto anime and manga series. The games feature a robust roster of characters and stunning visuals.

  4. Attack on Titan series:

    5. Based on the popular anime and manga, these games put players in control of the iconic Omni-Directional Mobility Gear as they battle giant titans.

  5. Persona 5 (2016):

    6. A role-playing game with an anime art style, combining dungeon-crawling with social simulation elements.

  6. Hero One's Justice series:

    7. Fighting games based on the My Hero Academia anime, featuring characters with unique superpowers known as Quirks.

  7. Sword Art Online series:

    8. Various games based on the Sword Art Online anime and light novel series, often combining elements of action RPG and virtual reality.

  8. Pokémon series:

    9. While not strictly an anime adaptation, Pokémon is a highly popular anime series, and the video games have been a staple of the anime culture for years.
  9. Tokyo Xanadu eX+(2017):

    10. An action RPG with anime aesthetics, blending dungeon exploration with real-time combat.

  10. Fire Emblem series:

    11. While not directly tied to an anime, Fire Emblem games often feature anime-style art and storytelling in a tactical role-playing game format. These are just a few examples, and there are many more anime-inspired games across different genres. Keep in mind that the availability of these games may vary depending on your gaming platform and region.
